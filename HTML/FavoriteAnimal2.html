Charizard Facts

Charizard flies around the sky in search of powerful opponents. It breathes fire of such great heat that it melts anything. However, it never turns its fiery breath on any opponent weaker than itself.Charizard is a Fire/Flying type Pokémon introduced in Generation. It is known as the Flame Pokémon.Charizard has two Mega Evolutions, available from X & Y onwards.
